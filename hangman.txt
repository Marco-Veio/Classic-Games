Objective:
	Guess the word chosen by the opponent.
How to play:
	The opponent chooses a word (in lower case), and you have to guess 1 letter each time.
	When you guess a letter correctly, it will appear at the correct quantity and position. If choose a wrong
	letter, it will be on the error list and you will lose a try.
Dificuldades:
	Easy: 10 tries
	Medium: 6 tries
	Hard: 3 tries
